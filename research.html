“Is Rich Phenomenology Fragmented?”
Synthese, 2025. [Springer]
This article argues that the richness of phenomenal experience does not imply unity. I offer a modular account of consciousness based on disunity across sensory modalities.

Work in Progress

“New Object Theory and Illusion”
To be presented at Eastern APA, 2026
Proposes a theory of perceptual objecthood that accounts for visual illusions without positing representational error. Influenced by relationalist views of perception.
“Do Semantic Properties Involve the Future?”
Pacific APA, 2025 (forthcoming)
Investigates whether the meaning of linguistic expressions can depend on future events. This has implications for semantic externalism and the metaphysics of reference.
