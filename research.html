“Is Rich Phenomenology Fragmented?”

Published in Synthese, 2025. This paper challenges the assumption that the richness of experience entails unity. I propose a modular account of consciousness grounded in disunified perceptual modalities. [Read]

“New Object Theory and Illusion”

Defends a novel account of perceptual objecthood that accommodates visual illusions without positing representational error. Influenced by relationalist theories of perception.

“Do Semantic Properties Involve the Future?”

Investigates whether the meaning of linguistic expressions depends on future-directed truth conditions. Discusses implications for semantic anti-realism and temporal metaphysics.
