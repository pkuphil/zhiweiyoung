“Is Rich Phenomenology Fragmented?”

Published in Synthese, 2025. This paper challenges the assumption that the richness of phenomenal experience entails unity. I propose a modular model of perceptual consciousness across modalities.
[Read on Springer]

“New Object Theory and Illusion”

This project develops a theory of perceptual objecthood that accommodates visual illusions without positing representational error. The approach draws on relationalism and the transparency of experience.

“Do Semantic Properties Involve the Future?”

I explore whether linguistic meaning depends on future-directed conditions. This touches on semantic anti-realism, temporal externalism, and the metaphysics of truth-conditions.
